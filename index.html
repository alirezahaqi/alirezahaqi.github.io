Research

Current focus

My recent work is centered on the design and analysis of algorithms where probability, discrete structure, and computation models interact.

Sampling and counting

Algorithms that turn structural or oracle access into faster high-accuracy sampling procedures.

Graph optimization

Matchings, spanning trees, cuts, and rounding questions motivated by graph algorithms and approximation.

Distributed algorithms

Fast randomized routines in constrained communication models such as the Broadcast Congested Clique.
